A Little about me and our Town

My name is Joe and I live with my wife, two sons, a Standard Poodle and a Himalayan Cat on Santino Court in Mahopac, NY. Mahopac is nestled in southern Putnam County, bordering Westchester County. If you do not like lakes, hills or nature then you should definitely not move to Mahopac! It is a hilly lake town with lots of hiking trails, historical sites and nature. Almost makes me think the absurd property tax balances out ... maybe. Below is an image from google maps showing the terrain of our town.

An interesting fact about Mahopac:

Frank Loyd Wright inspired house resides on a private island in the center of Lake Mahopac. For the insignificant sum of 10 million dollars (pre pandemic) you can own it! It comes with your very own helipad and a dock for your boat. Because its on an isalnd and submarine cars have not been invested yet, i think. Someone call Elon. Here is a link to Petra isalnd where you can learn more. The sprawling estate was originally designed by Wright, however the builder fell short financially. What was built instead would be a small 1500 sqft cottage. A later owner would hire a Wright expert and scholar to bring the vision to fruition through notes, drawings and diagrams. Long story short, if the zombie apocalypse ever happens i call dibs on the fancy pants island house.
