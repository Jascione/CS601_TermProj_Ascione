These are our pets, Cooper and Molly. Cooper is a standard Poodle who is just shy of two years old and Molly is our Himalayan Cat who just turned 10. For the most part they get along as well as a cat and a dog can be expected. Cooper wants to play 24/7 and Molly is all about being lazy with just enough inquisitiveness to taunt Cooper every so often. Something I learned about poodles when we bought Cooper is that they are large breed dogs, are very athletic and were bred to be water hunting dogs. This means Cooper needs plenty of outside time and luckily, we have a big enough yard and we put in an invisible fence to keep him safe but also give him the freedom to run around.
